RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

SCREEN SIZE          BEHAVIOR                   TOOLTIP POSITION
═══════════════════  ════════════════════════  ═════════════════════

> 768px              Hover to show             Above term
(Desktop/Tablet)     Move away to hide         (with arrow pointing down)
                     Keyboard accessible


< 768px              Tap to show               Below term
(Mobile)             Tap outside to hide       (with arrow pointing up)
                     Touch-optimized           Adjusted to screen


Print                Definitions inline        N/A (in brackets)
                     Example: [definition]     After term name
