POLICY PROPOSAL: AFFORDABLE HOUSING INITIATIVE

Candidate: Dave Biggers
Campaign: RunDaveRun
Date: November 18, 2025

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy proposes a comprehensive affordable housing initiative to address the housing crisis in our community. The plan includes:

1. Zoning Reform
   - Mixed-use development incentives
   - Density bonuses for affordable units
   - Streamlined permitting process

2. Financial Support
   - Down payment assistance for first-time buyers
   - Tax credits for developers building affordable housing
   - $50M housing trust fund

3. Community Land Trusts
   - Permanent affordability through CLT model
   - Community ownership and governance
   - Long-term price stability

BUDGET IMPACT
Estimated cost: $75 million over 5 years
Expected outcomes: 5,000 new affordable units

This policy aligns with our campaign's commitment to housing as a human right.
